Our Story

Learn more about who we are and what we stand for

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010, SoleStep began as a small shoe store in downtown Boston with a simple mission: to provide high-quality footwear that combines comfort, style, and durability.

Our founder, Michael Thompson, started the company after struggling to find shoes that met his standards as both a marathon runner and a fashion enthusiast. What began as a single store has now grown into a nationally recognized brand with over 50 locations across the country.

Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our core values: quality craftsmanship, exceptional customer service, and a commitment to innovation in footwear technology.

Our Mission

To empower every step with footwear that inspires confidence and comfort.

We believe that the right pair of shoes can transform how you move through the world. That's why we meticulously design and test every product to ensure it meets our high standards before it reaches your feet.

Our team of designers and engineers work tirelessly to incorporate the latest advancements in comfort technology while maintaining timeless style that transcends seasonal trends.
